about me

I make films for people who want their story told with honesty and care.

I'm Zhannah — a filmmaker and creative storyteller based between London and Kazakhstan. I work with communities, individuals, and organisations to make documentary-style films that feel true: events, cultural moments, travel, and the stories that are hard to put into words but easy to feel on screen.

My parents are ethnic Kazakhs who grew up in China before returning to Kazakhstan. Questions of home, culture, and belonging have always been close to me — and that shapes the way I notice people, what they carry, and the stories worth telling.

My background in sociology and psychology is part of what I bring to every project. I'm trained to pay attention — to culture, to emotion, to the things happening just beneath the surface. That's what makes a film feel true rather than just nice to look at.
